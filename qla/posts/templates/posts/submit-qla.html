Rules:

Here you can post things that make you feel happy, sad, surprised, angry, uplifted or lost on the journey of your quarter life adventure

  1. We have a number of banned topics. Please don't submit any of the following:

    • Personal information Posts including phone number, emails(which you can fill in optionally and we will keep it private for you), credit card information, bank account etc.
    • Violence. Content portraying violence, whether it be animal or human, are prohibited and will be removed.
    • Links to clickbait-esque sites. Any post linking to Buzzfeed or any other clickbait-esque site will be removed.

  2. Don't post NSFW links or images. NSFW text posts and comments are allowed.

  3. QLA reserve the right to remove content or restrict users' posting privileges as necessary, if it is deemed detrimental to the experience of others.

Please report rule-violating posts by messaging the editors with a link to the post in question. This helps us remove them more promptly.
